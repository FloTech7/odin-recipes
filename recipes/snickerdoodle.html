Snickerdoodle Cookie

Description

Here are quick and easy steps to make the best Snickerdoodle cookies you've ever had, right in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs, mix well.
  4. Add flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Roll dough into 1 inch balls.
  6. Mix sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl.
  7. Roll dough balls in cinnamon sugar mixture.
  8. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  10. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
