Birthday Cake Cookie

Description

Here are quick and easy steps to make the best birthday cake cookies you've ever had, right in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup sprinkles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, mix well.
  4. Add baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add flour, mix until combined.
  6. Stir in white chocolate chips and sprinkles.
  7. Use a cookie scoop to scoop out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  9. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.

Snickerdoodle Cookie

Description

Here are quick and easy steps to make the best Snickerdoodle cookies you've ever had, right in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Add eggs, mix well.
  4. Add flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Roll dough into 1 inch balls.
  6. Mix sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl.
  7. Roll dough balls in cinnamon sugar mixture.
  8. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
  10. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.

Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

Here are quick and easy steps to make the best Chocolate Chip Cookies you've ever had, right in your kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Cream together butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs, mix well.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture, mix well.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes.
  9. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
